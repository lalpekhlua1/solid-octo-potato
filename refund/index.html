    • At TitanNodes, we strive to provide high-quality Minecraft server hosting services. We understand that sometimes circumstances change, and you may need to request a refund. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which we provide refunds for our services.

    1. Eligibility for Refunds

    New Customers

    For new customers, we offer a 72-hour money-back guarantee from the time of initial purchase. If you're not satisfied with our service within this period, you may request a full refund.

    Existing Customers

    For existing customers who have been with us for more than 72 hours, refund eligibility depends on the circumstances:

    Reason Refund Eligibility Conditions
    Service Unavailability Partial Refund If our service experiences significant downtime (exceeding 5% in a month) not caused by scheduled maintenance
    Technical Issues Partial Refund If persistent technical issues prevent normal use of the service and cannot be resolved by our support team
    Billing Error Full Refund If you were charged incorrectly or multiple times for the same service period
    Service Cancellation No Refund For cancellations after the 72-hour period for reasons not related to service issues

    2. Non-Refundable Items

    The following items and circumstances are not eligible for refunds:

    • Domain name registrations and renewals
    • Add-on services once they have been activated
    • Setup fees
    • Services canceled after the 72-hour money-back guarantee period (except for qualifying circumstances)
    • Services terminated due to violations of our Terms of Service

    3. Refund Process

    To request a refund, please follow these steps:

    1. Contact our support team through the client area or Discord server
    2. Provide your account information and order number
    3. Explain the reason for your refund request
    4. Include any relevant information or evidence to support your request

    We will review your request and respond within 48 hours. If approved, refunds will be processed to the original payment method used for the purchase.

    4. Refund Processing Time

    Once a refund is approved:

    • Credit/debit card refunds typically take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement
    • PayPal refunds are usually processed within 1-3 business days
    • Other payment methods may vary in processing time

    5. Prorated Refunds

    For partial refunds due to service issues, we calculate the refund amount based on:

    • The number of days of service affected
    • The monthly cost of your plan
    • The severity and impact of the issue

    6. Service Cancellation

    Canceling your service does not automatically qualify you for a refund. To cancel your service:

    1. Log in to your client area
    2. Navigate to the services section
    3. Select the service you wish to cancel
    4. Click on the "Request Cancellation" button
    5. Follow the prompts to complete the cancellation process

    Services will remain active until the end of the current billing period unless otherwise specified.

    7. Special Circumstances

    We understand that special circumstances may arise. If you have a unique situation not covered by this policy, please contact our support team, and we will review your case individually.

    8. Changes to This Policy

    TitanNodes reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. It is your responsibility to review this policy periodically.

    9. Contact Us

    If you have any questions about our Refund Policy, please contact us through our Discord server at discord.gg/titannodes or support channels available on our website.
